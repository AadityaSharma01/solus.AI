 Sure! Here's a simple veggie omelette recipe:

Ingredients:
- 3 eggs
- Salt, to taste
- Pepper, to taste
- 2 tablespoons of olive oil
- 1/2 cup of mixed vegetables (like bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, spinach)
- Cheese (optional)

Instructions:
1. Beat the eggs in a bowl and season with salt and pepper. Set aside.
2. Heat the olive oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
3. Add your mixed vegetables and sauté until they are tender.
4. Pour the beaten eggs over the vegetables in the pan, stirring gently to spread them evenly.
5. Let the omelette cook for about 2 minutes or until the bottom is set.
6. If using cheese, sprinkle it evenly over half of the omelette.
7. Carefully fold the other half of the omelette over the filled side.
8. Cook for another minute or two, then slide the omelette onto a plate and serve.

Enjoy your veggie omelette!
